Office Administration Program

About the Course

The Bachelor of Science in Office Administration (BSOA) prepares students to become skilled professionals in managing office operations, handling business communications, and supporting administrative systems.

Key Topics Covered

  • Office Procedures and Practices
  • Business Communication and Correspondence
  • Records Management and Filing Systems
  • Computer Applications in Business
  • Office Management and Supervision

Importance of Office Administration

Every organization — from small businesses to large corporations — relies on efficient administration. It ensures smooth workflow, proper documentation, effective communication, and overall productivity. Without good administration, operations become disorganized and inefficient.

Career Opportunities

  • Administrative Assistant / Officer
  • Office Manager / Supervisor
  • Executive Assistant
  • Records Management Officer
  • Front Office Coordinator
  • Administrative Support Specialist
